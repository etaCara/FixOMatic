❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📌 How do I submit a new ticket?

Click on the “Create” tab in the navigation bar. Fill in the issue details and submit the form.

🔍 Where can I see the status of my tickets?

You can view your open and closed tickets in the "History" tab. Each ticket will show its current status.

👤 Who can see my tickets?

Only you and the IT team have access to your ticket details. Admins may have oversight as well.

🔐 I forgot my password. What should I do?

Currently, password reset is not available in demo mode. Please contact an admin for help.

📆 How long will it take to resolve my issue?

Resolution time depends on the severity of the issue. Critical tickets are prioritized by the IT team.
